PRIVACY POLICY

This is Eteral's website. If you are only browsing this website, we do not capture data that allows us to identify you individually. We collect information from our users to better understand our users and their needs.

Below is our policy on data collection and use of the data collected.

When is information collected from you?

When you submit an enquiry form or email us.

What is the information used for?

To perform obligations in the course of or in connection with our provision of the goods and/or services requested by you

To verify your identity

To respond to and process queries, requests, applications, complaints, and feedback from you

To manage your relationship with us

To process payment or credit transactions

To comply with any applicable laws, regulations, codes of practice, guidelines, or rules, or to assist in law enforcement and investigations conducted by any governmental and/or regulatory authority

And for any other purposes for which you have provided the information

Will your personal information be shared with anyone?

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. As a general rule, we do not disclose specific information about our users to any third parties unless required by law or any government agencies. All communication from us will be sent to you by us.

How your data is protected?

To safeguard your personal data from unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal or similar risks, we have introduced appropriate administrative, physical and technical measures such as minimised collection of personal data, authentication and access controls (such as good password practices, need-to-basis for data disclosure, etc.), encryption of data, data anonymisation, up-to-date antivirus protection, regular patching of operating system and other software, securely erase storage media in devices before disposal, web security measures against risks, and security review and testing performed regularly.

Use of your Personal Data

The disclosure of personal information is always done so voluntarily. Your name, address, phone number, and email address are examples of personal data. Please be aware that processing sales inquiries, for instance, may entail the use of part of this data. Any personal information you give will only be used to respond to your inquiry.

The integrity and security of your personal information are upheld by Eteral as part of its commitment to safeguarding your privacy. In accordance with the guidelines of the Personal Data Protection Act, we save personal data. Your personal information won't be distributed to parties outside Eteral by way of sale, loan, or any other means.

All electronic communication and storage of personal data is safeguarded for your privacy.
